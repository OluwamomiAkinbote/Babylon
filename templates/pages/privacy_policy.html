Commenting Policy

Scodynate News invites readers to comment but requires civility and constructive dialogue. The site uses the Facebook Comments Plugin, which collects personal information, and logging in via Facebook is mandatory for participation.

Comments are mostly post-moderated and can be removed if they violate guidelines. Users can report inappropriate comments using a drop-down menu, and moderators will review flagged content. Violations can also be reported to Facebook.

Some stories may have disabled comments due to legal concerns. The site enforces a zero-tolerance policy for spam and commercial promotions, promptly banning offenders. Readers are encouraged to mark spam comments for moderation review. For data handling details, users can Facebook Data Policy.
